ABOUT THE EVENT

A Community
Comes Together

On September 12th, 2026, the community of Gilberts, Illinois will gather at Saint Peter Lutheran Church for a day rooted in remembrance, resilience, and the enduring American spirit.

America Strong Family Fest is a free, public event welcoming neighbors of every age — from the youngest children to our most seasoned community members. Come share a day of unity, entertainment, and heartfelt connection.
