About the Event

A Community
Comes Together

On September 12th, 2026, the community of Gilberts, Illinois comes together at St. Peter Lutheran Church for a joyful day of celebration, friendship, and the very best of the American spirit.

America Strong Family Fest is a free, public event welcoming neighbors of every age — from the youngest children to our more seasoned community members. We can’t wait to share a day full of music, laughter, great food, and new friendships. Bring your family and join us!

What to Expect

Something For
Everyone

A full day of live entertainment, food, community, and activities designed for every member of the family — from the youngest to the more seasoned.

🎵

Live Music

Patriotic performances and community entertainment filling the afternoon with American spirit and live sound that brings everyone together.

🍽

Food & Refreshments

Delicious food and refreshments to enjoy throughout the day, celebrating the very best flavors of Gilberts and the surrounding area.

🎨

Kids Zone

Games, crafts, and hands-on activities specially designed to delight our youngest community members and create lasting memories.

🤝

Community Connection

Meet local organizations, first responders, veterans groups, and connect with neighbors old and new in the spirit of resilience and remembrance.
