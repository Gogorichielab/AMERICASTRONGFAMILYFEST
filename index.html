About the Event

A Community
Comes Together

On September 12th, 2026, the community of Gilberts, Illinois will gather at Saint Peter Lutheran Church for a day rooted in remembrance, resilience, and the enduring American spirit.

America Strong Family Fest is a free, public event welcoming neighbors of every age — from the youngest children to our most seasoned community members. Come share a day of unity, entertainment, and heartfelt connection.

What to Expect

Something For
Everyone

A full day of live entertainment, food, community, and activities designed for every member of the family — from the youngest to the most seasoned.

01
🎵

Live Music

Patriotic performances and community entertainment filling the afternoon with American spirit and live sound that brings everyone together.

02
🍽

Food & Vendors

Local food vendors, artisan crafts, and community booths showcasing the very best of Gilberts and the surrounding area.

03
🎨

Kids Zone

Games, crafts, and hands-on activities specially designed to delight our youngest community members and create lasting memories.

04
🤝

Community Connection

Meet local organizations, first responders, veterans groups, and connect with neighbors old and new in the spirit of unity.
