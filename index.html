About the Event

America Strong Family Fest is a free, family-friendly community celebration held each year in remembrance of September 11th — a day that reminded us how strong, resilient, and united we are as Americans.

Inspired by the courage of first responders, the sacrifice of service members, and the unbreakable spirit of our communities, this event brings neighbors together to celebrate what makes America truly great: family, faith, and freedom.

Join us for a full day of live entertainment, delicious food, exciting activities for the kids, and the chance to connect with the incredible people who make our community shine.
